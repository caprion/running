Top Differentiators (What No Competitor Has)

Consistency Floor Tracking

RED/YELLOW/GREEN volume floors with streak tracking. Strava, Garmin, Nike, Runna -- none have this. Reddit runners constantly ask for it.

Pace Degradation Analysis

Measures how pace fades in long runs (first quarter vs last quarter). Strava has Best Efforts but not within-run degradation. Advanced coaching insight.

Historical Collapse Prediction

Risk calendar based on 4 years of your own data showing when training collapses historically happen. Completely unique to your running patterns.

Race Readiness Checklist

4-point readiness score with specific criteria (pace sustained, long runs done, volume at race pace). No app offers this structured readiness check.

Season-over-Season Comparison

Compare training blocks across seasons with quality session counts, violation rates, and volume trends. Garmin Connect+ only does basic time comparison.

Plan vs Actual Compliance

20-week plan tracking with phase, target volume, actual volume, and status per week. Runna adapts plans but doesn't show compliance history like this.

AI Run Overview (New Feature Design)

What It Does

A new dashboard page that uses Copilot agents to generate natural-language analysis of your runs, weekly trends, and training trajectory. Replaces the static Weekly Logs and generic Recovery recommendations.

Data Sources

  • Per-run analysis: Read from unified-cache.json -- distance, pace, HR, cadence, splits, elevation
  • Weekly trends: Aggregate last 4 weeks -- volume trend, consistency status, quality session count
  • Season context: Current plan phase, compliance %, upcoming key workouts, risk level
  • Historical context: Same week last season comparison, PR proximity, floor violations

Sample AI Output

This Week (Week 6, Feb 10-16): 32.4km across 4 runs. Volume is tracking at 94% of plan (target: 34km). Your Thursday tempo at 5:28/km was the highlight -- 5s faster than your Week 4 tempo at similar HR (152 bpm), suggesting genuine fitness gains rather than just pace-chasing.

Trend: Rolling 4-week average is 31.2km, up from 26.8km four weeks ago. You've had 5 consecutive GREEN weeks. Cadence has improved from 162 to 166 spm in easy runs.

Watch for: Your long run HR drift was 8.2% (target: under 5%). Consider starting 10-15s/km slower. Saturday's long run averaged 5:52/km -- similar effort at 6:05/km would keep drift under control.

Implementation Path

  • Phase 1: Add a "generate AI overview" button to the dashboard that calls a Python script
  • Phase 2: Script reads unified-cache.json, computes metrics, formats prompt for Copilot agent
  • Phase 3: Agent returns structured analysis (run summary, trend, watch-for items)
  • Phase 4: Cache the generated overview in .github/framework/artifacts/ for quick reload
